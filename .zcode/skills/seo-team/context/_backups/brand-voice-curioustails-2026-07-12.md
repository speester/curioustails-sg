# Brand Voice: Curious Tails

## Tone in three words

Honest. Educational. Caring.

The site says "We'd rather talk you out of the wrong breed than sell you the wrong dog" — this defines the tone: putting the customer's and puppy's wellbeing ahead of a quick sale.

## The reader

- **Who's reading:** Singaporean adults (working couples, families, retirees, first-time dog owners) searching for a puppy to bring home
- **Reading level:** plain English + technical depth when it matters ("Pet Purchase Declaration", "vaccination guidelines")
- **What they're afraid of:** Will the puppy fit my lifestyle? Is this a dodgy online seller or a real, safe, licensed breeder? What are the real costs? Will I mess up the first days? Is this the right breed?

## Say / Don't say

| Always | Never |
|--------|-------|
| Lifestyle-first matching ("who is home, when?") | One-size-fits-all breed recommendations |
| "AVS licensed" + link to public registry, licence number visible | "Registered" (vague), "family breeder" (unverifiable) |
| Transparent, itemized pricing + what is included | Hidden costs, surprise fees, "call for pricing" |
| "We board your puppy for free until you're ready" | Pressure the sale, rush to delivery |
| Real case studies with names (Andrew Mak, Jaden Chua) | Generic testimonials or made-up outcomes |
| "If your puppy falls sick within 5 days, bring them back and we'll nurse them back" | Generic "health guarantee" without detail |
| Acknowledge Singapore-specific constraints (HDB rules, smuggling risk) | Pretend these don't exist |
| Plain English + technical depth when it matters | Jargon without translation, or baby-talk dumbing down |

## Sentence style

- **Person:** "We", "our" (Nelson and Kim as collective voice), occasional "I" when personal
- **Contractions:** yes, casual ("won't", "it's", "don't")
- **Humour:** gentle, situational — warm and knowing, not stand-up ("no overwhelming introductions yet", "whimpering is a normal part of adjusting")

## Words from the client's own mouth

"We'd rather talk you out of the wrong breed than sell you the wrong dog."

"From $3,288, all-in — starter kit, home delivery and first training lesson included."

"Curious Tails is run by two people you will actually meet: Nelson and Kim, AVS licensed (AS24J00046)."
