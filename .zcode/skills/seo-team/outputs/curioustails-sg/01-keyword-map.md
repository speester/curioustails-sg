## Executive Summary

Curious Tails operates in a high-intent market with **1,000+ monthly searches** for core keywords like "puppy for sale singapore." However, the domain is currently absent from top-50 organic rankings for these main keywords, indicating a significant SEO opportunity. The competitor landscape shows that niche breed keywords (Cavapoo, Maltipoo) and long-tail service queries are underserved, creating an immediate path to quick wins while building authority for primary keywords.

**Key Strategic Insight:** The market is not keyword-starved; competitors dominate head-term volume. Curious Tails' differentiation (AVS licensing, free training, HDB breeds, 5-star reviews) should anchor content in long-tail queries that reflect these assets.
