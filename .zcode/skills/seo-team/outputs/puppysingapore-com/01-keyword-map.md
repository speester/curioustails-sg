# Keyword Map: Puppy Singapore (2026-07-12)

Data source: DataForSEO (Keyword Ideas, Keyword Suggestions, Related Keywords, Keyword Overview, Ranked Keywords for puppysingapore.com + 3 competitors, SERP/PAA). Location: Singapore. Language: English. All volumes are monthly, Singapore-only.

Strategy lens applied throughout: this domain's angle is **topical authority / buyer's-guide content**, not hyperlocal/GBP. Breed "for sale" and core transactional terms remain in scope (they are still the money keywords), but the priority tilt is toward buying-guide, comparison, cost/ownership, and breed-education content — the same lane a genuine competitor (prettypetskennel.com) is already running successfully.

## Executive summary

- **The core money page is winning already.** The homepage ranks positions 2–15 for `puppy for sale singapore` (1,000 vol), `puppy for sale sg` (1,000), `dog for sale sg` (1,000), and dozens of `buy/where to buy [dog/puppy] singapore` variants — this is the strongest asset on the domain and should be defended, not rebuilt.
- **~30 breed pages exist and most rank top-20**, but almost none of them pair a "for sale" page with a companion buying-guide/cost/ownership page — which is exactly the gap the strongest competitor, prettypetskennel.com, is exploiting breed-by-breed (e.g. "How Much Does It Cost to Own a Corgi in Singapore," "Owning A Dachshund In Singapore").
- **The single biggest number on the map is `dog adoption singapore` / `dog for adoption singapore` at 4,400 searches/month each**, and the client already ranks position ~5 for both — a realistic path to page-1 top-3 with focused work, not a new-content project.
- **Whole buying-guide topics are wide open**: dog breed comparison hubs, "types of poodles / poodle mixes," dog license Singapore, pet insurance Singapore, low-maintenance/hypoallergenic breeds for HDB flats, and ethical-breeder vs. puppy-mill trust content are all ranking for a competitor and have zero or thin coverage on puppysingapore.com.
- **Search volume for most breed+price/for-sale terms is small and declining** (yearly trends of -20% to -60% on many corgi/golden retriever/pomeranian price queries) — be honest that individual breed-price keywords are low-volume; the win is the aggregate long-tail across 40+ breeds plus the buying-guide layer, not any single high-volume breed term.

## Local modifiers

**This domain's strategy explicitly excludes hyperlocal/GBP-style content** (that positioning is reserved for Curious Tails and wgpetfarm.com, both of which have GBPs). The findings below are noted for awareness, not as targets for puppysingapore.com:

- The core SERP for `puppy for sale singapore` fires a **local_pack** block (3 businesses: The Lovely Pets, Wellfond Pets Thomson, Wag A Tail Singapore — all with GBP ratings/reviews) ahead of organic results. Competitors with GBPs are capturing that slot; puppysingapore.com has no GBP by design and cannot compete there — this is a known, accepted tradeoff, not a gap to fix.
- No "[breed] puppy [neighbourhood/area]" or "near me" keyword showed meaningful volume in any of the pulls — the addressable demand in this space is genuinely national/delivery-based, which supports the buying-guide strategy over a hyperlocal one.
- `pet shop at serangoon north` (480/mo, competitor thelovelypets.com ranks pos 9) is the one quasi-local term that surfaced — flagged here only as a data point; out of scope to pursue for puppysingapore.com.
