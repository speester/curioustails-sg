# Performance Snapshot: Puppy Singapore (2026-07-12)

## Where they stand

- **407 keywords ranked** in Google Singapore (English), worth an estimated **$2,109/mo in traffic value** (ETV) and **$1,643/mo** if bought as paid search (DataForSEO Labs, 2026-07-12 snapshot).
- Position distribution is bottom-heavy: only **21 keywords in the top 3** and **46 in positions 4-10**. The other **80%+ of ranked keywords sit at position 11 or worse** (60 at 11-20, 280 at 21+), where almost no clicks land.
- **Real Google Search Console data (first-party, ground truth) shows a severe, sustained decline**: 16 months of GSC data show clicks and impressions falling steadily since roughly Dec 2024/Jan 2025, steepening further around Jan 2026 — traffic is down an estimated **70-80% from peak**. DataForSEO's own trend (Feb-Jul 2026 window only) agrees in direction — ETV fell from 3,625 to 2,109 (-42%) in just the last 6 months — but that window starts well after the GSC-documented collapse began, so DataForSEO understates the total damage; GSC is the trend to lead with.
- Traffic is **concentrated on the homepage and a handful of breed pages** (golden retriever, toy poodle, corgi) — not spread across the 40+ breed catalog. The homepage alone accounted for 3,887 of the last 6 months' clicks; most individual breed pages contribute a few hundred clicks or fewer. This concentration is a risk (a handful of pages carry the whole site) but also the leverage point (fixing/expanding those pages moves the needle fastest).
- **No manual actions** in GSC — the decline is not a penalty. Combined with the sharpest position collapses being on `/all-available-puppies/` (avg. position 33.9, down from 6.2) and `/dog-adoption-singapore/` (54.3, down from 7.1), this points to a technical/content/internal-linking cause on specific pages rather than a site-wide algorithmic hit.

## Traffic value estimate

**$2,109/mo estimated organic traffic value (ETV)**, or **$1,643/mo** if that same traffic were bought via paid search (`estimated_paid_traffic_cost`) — both from DataForSEO Labs `domain_rank_overview` for puppysingapore.com, Singapore/English, calculated as the sum of each ranked keyword's estimated traffic volume x its CPC. Annualized, that's roughly **$19,700-$25,300/yr** in organic value at current rankings — down from an estimated $3,625 ETV/mo just 6 months ago (Feb 2026), and down further still from the pre-decline peak GSC shows (70-80% off peak, a decline this DataForSEO window doesn't fully capture since it only starts in Feb 2026).

## Baseline for reporting

Re-measure these 5 metrics monthly to track recovery:

1. **GSC clicks + impressions** (primary — first-party, most trustworthy). Current: severe multi-month decline, no manual action.
2. **DataForSEO ETV / estimated traffic value** for puppysingapore.com. Current: $2,109/mo (down from $3,625/mo six months prior).
3. **Position distribution** (% of keywords in top-3 / 4-10 / 11-20 / 21+). Current: 5% / 11% / 15% / 69% — the top-3 share is the number to watch, since that's where competitors are winning.
4. **Striking-distance keyword count** (rank 4-20) and how many convert into top-10. Current: ~60+ keywords in this band, homepage-heavy.
5. **Total ranked keyword count**. Current: 407 — roughly at parity with competitors, so this metric alone won't show the real story; pair it with #3.
