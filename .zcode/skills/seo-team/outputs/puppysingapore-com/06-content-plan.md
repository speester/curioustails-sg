## Why this order

- **#1 (dog adoption) isn't a new blog piece** — it's an existing 1,700+ word page with genuine FAQ content already ranking position ~5 for two 4,400-volume terms. `02-technical-audit.md` found a dual-H1 template bug across the whole adoption cluster and zero FAQPage schema despite real FAQ H3s already on the page. That's an on-page/technical fix, not a content-writing job, so it's listed here for sequencing visibility but handed to the On-Page Copywriter role rather than drafted in this file. It stays priority 1 because volume-to-effort ratio beats every other item on this list by a wide margin.
- **#2 (puppy cost guide) is the highest-priority piece this role actually writes.** It's the single most direct fix for the GEO audit's #1 citability gap — puppysingapore.com publishes no price anywhere on-site while every visible competitor snippet leads with a dollar figure. A well-sourced, honestly-framed aggregate guide gives AI Overviews one canonical page to cite for "how much does a puppy cost in Singapore" even before the owner decides whether to publish the site's own number (see `> NEEDS FROM YOU` markers in the draft).
- **#3–#4 (breed-cost companions)** ride the existing authority of `/corgi/` and `/golden-retriever/`, both already breed pages with real traffic and (for Corgi) an existing AI Overview citation — mirrors prettypetskennel.com's proven format from the keyword map's competitor-gap analysis.
- **#5 (breed hub)** becomes the internal-link spine once 2–4 companion pieces exist to link into it — sequenced after the cost pieces so it launches with real content behind it, not empty links.
- **#6 (comparison post)** is fast to produce and a natural link bridge between `/cavapoo/` and future Maltipoo coverage; lowest lift, so it closes out the month.

## Publishing cadence

Realistic for a one-person operation (owner is also the SEO lead): **one piece every 5–7 days**,
starting with #2 once the owner answers the `> NEEDS FROM YOU` questions in the draft (pricing
decision is the long pole — everything else in the draft can ship without it, just softer).
Suggested calendar:

- Week 1: Finalize #2 (pending pricing decision), publish.
- Week 2: #3 (Corgi cost/ownership).
- Week 3: #4 (Golden Retriever cost/ownership).
- Week 4: #5 (Dog Breeds hub) — launch with internal links into #2–#4 already live.
- Week 5 (into next month): #6 (Cavapoo vs Maltipoo) + start #1's technical handoff verification.

Do not batch-publish all 6 in one week — Google and AI crawlers re-index gradually, and staggering
gives time to check whether the pricing decision (once made) actually moves the AI Overview
citation count before committing to a site-wide Product/Offer schema rollout.
