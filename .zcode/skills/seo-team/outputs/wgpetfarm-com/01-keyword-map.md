# Keyword Map: William Goh Pet Farm (2026-07-12)

Data source: DataForSEO (Google, Singapore, English), live pulls 2026-07-13. Client: wgpetfarm.com — physical-premises pet farm/breeder at Block L, 59 Sungei Tengah Rd, with a claimed GBP (4.5★, 64 reviews). Unlike sister property puppysingapore.com, this domain is designed to own the **hyperlocal / local-pack / "visit the farm" lane**, so local modifiers are treated as a core cluster here, not excluded.

## Executive summary

- **wgpetfarm.com already ranks #2 in the Google local pack for "pet farm singapore"** (210 vol/mo, live-checked), right behind a nature-experience centre and ahead of a well-known goat farm — the hyperlocal positioning is real and working, not aspirational. It also organically ranks page 1 for "pets farm singapore" (rank 4), "sungei tengah dog farm" (rank 3), "dog farm in singapore" (rank 8), and "dog farm sg" (rank 9). This is the domain's strongest, most defensible cluster and should be doubled down on with dedicated location/visit content.
- The site already has a real breed-page architecture (`/corgi-puppies-for-sale/`, `/golden-retriever-puppies-for-sale/`, `/yorkshire-terrier-puppies-for-sale/`, `/maltipoo-puppies-for-sale/`, `/border-collie-puppies-for-sale/`, `/pomsky-puppies-for-sale/`, `/japanese-spitz-puppies-for-sale/`, `/labrador-retriever-puppies-for-sale/`, `/bichon-puppies-for-sale/`, `/chow-chow-puppies-for-sale/`, `/poodle-puppies-for-sale/`) and several of these rank page 1 for long-tail breed+price queries — this is an on-page refresh/expansion job, not a build-from-zero job.
- The single biggest missed opportunity found: **"vet near me" / "vets near me" (9,900 searches/mo each) rank position 69–98** on the site's `/24-hour-vet-clinics/` page — miles off page 1 despite being by far the highest-volume terms the domain touches anywhere. Similarly, the site's own location term **"sungei tengah" (720 vol) ranks only position 44**, on `/contact-us/` — the domain is under-optimized for its own address, which is the core hyperlocal asset.
- Competitor gap: **Pretty Pets Kennel holds the #1 local pack slot for "dog breeder singapore"** (260 vol) and **The Lovely Pets holds #1 for "puppy for sale singapore"** (1,000 vol) — wgpetfarm.com does not currently contest either local pack. Both competitors also have dedicated breed pages (Cavapoo, Dachshund, Maltese, Poodle, French Bulldog, Samoyed, Pug, etc.) ranking positions 2–45 for 1,000–9,900-volume national breed terms — a wide-open mid-volume opportunity zone neither competitor has fully claimed either.
- Core transactional head terms are large but tough: "puppy for sale singapore" / "puppies for sale singapore" (1,000 vol each, KD 12–15, HIGH competition) are local-pack-gated (Lovely Pets/Cotton Pups/Pup Life own the pack); "dog breeder singapore" (260 vol, KD 5–13) is a more winnable head term where Pretty Pets Kennel's local-pack lead is the main obstacle, not organic difficulty.
