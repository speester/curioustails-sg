# Performance Snapshot: William Goh Pet Farm (2026-07-12)

## Where they stand

- **The branded-vs-non-branded gap is the story.** In the DataForSEO keyword set (996 ranked keywords), only **3 keywords (0.3% of the total, ~0.1% of total search volume)** are branded ("william goh pet farm," "william goh kennel," "william goh pet farm reviews"). All three rank at position 1-3. The other **993 keywords (99.7%) are non-branded category/breed terms**, and the large majority of those sit outside page 1. This is the mirror image of the GSC data: branded position 2.0 (excellent) vs. overall average position 39.8 (weak) — the site wins decisively on its own name and struggles everywhere else.
- **Only ~11% of ranking keywords are on page 1.** Domain-level position buckets: 3 keywords at #1, 19 at #2-3, 33 at #4-10 — 55 of 495 tracked keywords (11.1%) are page-1. The remaining ~89% are scattered from page 2 through page 9+ (761 of 996 keywords in the fuller keyword-level pull sit at rank 21+).
- **Traffic value is trending down, not up, over the last 6 months**, even as the number of ranking keywords held roughly steady. Estimated traffic value (ETV) fell from 4,250 (Feb 2026) to 1,880 (Jul 2026) — a 56% drop — while keyword count actually rose slightly (420 → 495). That means existing keywords are sliding down in position even as new, low-value ones get picked up. This reconciles with the real GSC trend (traffic down, less steeply than puppysingapore.com) and points to erosion inside the SERP, not a loss of topical footprint.
- **One keyword, "pug breeders" (rank #3, 5,400 searches/mo), drives ~19% of the site's entire non-branded traffic value** — the portfolio is thin at the top; losing or gaining that single keyword swings the numbers materially.
- **Against physical-premises competitors, wgpetfarm.com is competitive on breadth but weak on ranking quality.** It has more ranked keywords than Pretty Pets Kennel and The Lovely Pets, but only 11% of them are page-1, versus 44% for The Lovely Pets — see benchmark table below.

**Reading this table honestly:** wgpetfarm.com is not losing to Pretty Pets Kennel — it's ahead there. The real gap is The Lovely Pets, which ranks fewer keywords overall but wins far more of the ones that matter (44% page-1 vs. 11%). That is a ranking-quality problem on wgpetfarm.com's existing content, not a content-coverage problem — the breed pages already exist (per the striking-distance list above, most are sitting at #9-20), they're just not winning the SERP.

## Traffic value estimate

**$1,215.92/month** — DataForSEO's `estimated_paid_traffic_cost` for wgpetfarm.com, i.e. what the site's current organic rankings would cost to replicate via paid search at Singapore CPCs. Cross-check: `etv` (estimated organic visits/month at current positions) = 1,879.55.

This has fallen from a 6-month high of $2,640.68/mo (May 2026) — a decline consistent with, though less steep than, the real GSC traffic trend described for this domain.

## Baseline for reporting

Re-measure these 5 metrics monthly to track progress:

1. **Branded vs. non-branded keyword share** — currently 3 branded / 993 non-branded keywords ranked; watch whether non-branded page-1 count grows.
2. **Page-1 keyword count and %** — currently 55 keywords / 11.1% (domain_rank_overview basis).
3. **Estimated traffic value (ETV)** — currently 1,879.55 (down from 4,250 in Feb 2026; watch for the decline to flatten or reverse).
4. **Striking-distance conversion rate** — currently 207 non-branded keywords at rank 4-20; track how many move into page-1 each month, starting with "maltipoo singapore" (#4), "golden retriever puppy" (#9), "poodle mix breeders" (#9).
5. **GSC overall average position** — currently 39.8 (16-month baseline); this is the single number that should move if the striking-distance work is landing.
