## H2: Do I need an appointment to visit William Goh Pet Farm?

**Capsule:** Yes. Viewing is strictly by appointment, every time, no walk-ins — you book a visit in advance via WhatsApp before coming to see any puppy in person.

This isn't a soft preference, it's stated plainly across the site: "Viewing Strictly By Appointment." The reason is practical as much as anything: puppies are on a working farm premises, not in a retail shopfront, so visits are scheduled to make sure a puppy is ready to be seen (and that someone's actually there to show you around) rather than dropping in unannounced.

The fastest way to book: message the farm directly and ask to arrange a viewing for a specific breed or puppy you've already spotted on the [puppies-for-sale page](/puppies-for-sale/). Most listings on the site repeat the same call to action — "View More Photos & Video on WhatsApp" — because that's genuinely the fastest path from browsing to booking.
