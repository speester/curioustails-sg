## Verified Breed Facts

### Characteristics
- **Hybrid:** Cavalier King Charles Spaniel × Toy Poodle
- **Size (Curious Tails first-party):** Toy Cavapoo 3–5 kg; Mini Cavapoo 5–10 kg (mini is the more common size). ~30 cm tall
- **Coat:** Wavy to curly; low-shedding
- **Temperament:** Affectionate, loyal, intelligent, playful
- **Exercise:** Moderate; 30–60 min daily
- **Lifespan:** 12–15 years
- **Source:** Pretty Pets Kennel (AVS breeder guide); Blog.petloverscentre.com

**Reasoning:** Cavapoos are more independent; Maltipoos need more daily human contact and tend to whine when alone

## Health & Care (Verified)

- **Vaccinations required:** weeks 6 / 8 / 12 core course (optional 4th at week 16 if the vet advises); deworming together with EVERY vaccination visit; puppy goes home ~1 week after the 2nd jab (around week 9); ANNUAL booster + deworming from Year 1 onward — NOT a one-time "Year 2" event. Canonical source: business-context.md "Singapore Regulatory & Health Facts" (corrected by hand 2026-07; never regress to 6/9/12)
- **Microchipping:** Mandatory in Singapore; done by vet
- **HDB rules (per owner, 2026-07):** Cavapoo IS an HDB-approved small breed; CT places them in HDB flats. One dog of an approved breed per flat. Other HDB-approved breeds CT carries: Maltipoo, Bichonpoo, Mini Dachshund, Bichon, Chihuahua.
- **Common issues:** Ear infections (floppy ears), eye problems (inherited), patellar luxation
- **Food:** Small-breed puppy formula; introduce Curious Tails' included food gradually
- **Grooming:** Brush 3–4x weekly; professional grooming every 6–8 weeks
- **Sources:** AVS guidelines; Pawrenthood Singapore; Royal Canin
