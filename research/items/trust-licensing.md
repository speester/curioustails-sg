## Curious Tails: AVS Licensed

### License Verification
- **License Number:** ACRA 202420075D
- **Verify here:** https://avs.nparks.gov.sg/outreach/resources/public-registry-of-avs-licensed-pet-shops/
- **What this means:**
  - Health & welfare standards inspected
  - Veterinary oversight
  - Vaccination requirements enforced
  - Microchipping standards met
  - Pet Purchase Declaration (PPD) form required (buyer suitability check)

### Certification Standard
- **Issuer:** Animal & Veterinary Service (AVS), National Parks Board
- **Requirement:** Mandatory training on pet welfare + animal management
- **Provider:** Temasek Polytechnic's Temasek SkillsFuture Academy (AVS-recognized)
- **Renewal:** Annual compliance checks
- **Source:** AVS Licensing page — https://avs.nparks.gov.sg/businesses/pet-shop-owners/pet-shop-licence/

## How to Verify ANY Pet Shop

**Step 1:** Go to AVS Public Registry  
https://avs.nparks.gov.sg/outreach/resources/public-registry-of-avs-licensed-pet-shops/

**Step 2:** Search by business name or address

**Step 3:** Check:
- ✅ License number visible?
- ✅ Current year listed?
- ✅ License matches their claims?

**Step 4:** If NOT listed = unlicensed = risk
